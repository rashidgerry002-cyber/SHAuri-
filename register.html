Registration
Complete your details on the SHA portal at sha.go.ke
💡Use Google Chrome or Firefox. The SHA portal has issues on Safari. Clear your browser cache if you've tried before and it failed.
🪪

Basic Information

SHA automatically pulls your basic info from government databases using your ID number. You must also supply employment status, monthly income, and disability status. Be truthful and accurate.

National ID / Passport / Alien Card

Go to sha.go.ke → Self Registration → enter your ID number to begin. Your basic info auto-fills.

Employment Status

Select: Formally Employed, Self-Employed, or Unemployed / Indigent — this determines your contribution rate (2.75% of gross salary or min Ksh 500/month).

Monthly Income

Required for contribution calculation. Be honest — underreporting is an offence.

Disability Status (if applicable)

Declaring a disability may qualify you for additional benefits or contribution subsidies.

📍

Contact & Address Information

This determines your Primary Care Network (PCN) — the hospitals assigned to you near your home.

Primary & Alternative Phone Numbers

An OTP verification will be sent to your primary number. Must be active and in your name.

Email Address

Used for confirmation and account recovery. Recommended but not mandatory.

County → Sub-County → Ward

Use your actual home area so your PCN hospitals are close to where you live.

✅

Confirm Details & Submit

Review all entries carefully

The portal lets you review and edit before final submission. Double check your name, ID number, and phone.

Make declaration and submit

After submission you get your Application Reference Number — screenshot this immediately! You need it for biometric verification.

🌐

⚠️ Registration Problems?

❌ "ID not found" error on the portal

▾
Double-check your ID number for typos. If correct, your ID may not be in the Huduma/IPRS database yet. Call SHA: 0800 720 601 for manual verification, or visit the nearest SHA office with your original ID.

❌ OTP not arriving on my phone

▾
Wait 5 minutes and try again. Check your signal strength. Make sure the number entered is correct and active. If still nothing, try a different browser. Call SHA: 0800 720 601 for manual OTP bypass.

❌ Portal keeps crashing or timing out

▾
Try at night (8pm–11pm) when traffic is lower. Use Chrome Incognito mode and clear all cookies. If completely down, wait 24 hours — SHA maintenance windows are common on weekdays 9am–11am.

❌ "Already registered" but I have no card

▾
Someone may have registered using your ID (old NHIF records or employer). Call SHA immediately: 0800 720 601 and request account ownership verification with your original ID.

Adding a Spouse

Proof of Marriage — ONE of these:

Marriage Certificate, or a Sworn Affidavit, or a Letter from your Chief confirming the marriage.

Spouse's Active Phone Number

An OTP is sent to their phone for consent. They must approve the addition themselves.

Spouse's National ID Number

Required to create their profile in the SHA system and link them to your account.

👶

Adding a Biological Child

📅Documents required depend on the child's age. Select which applies to your child:

Under 18 years

Provide Birth Certificate Number or Birth Notification Reference Number.

Age 18 and above

Provide the child's National ID Number.

Age 21–25 in full-time education

Proof of current enrollment — school admission letter or active student ID card.

Over 25 and disabled

Can only be added if disabled and under your care. Provide disability documentation (NCPWD card or medical certificate).

🤝

Adding an Adopted Child

Official Adoption Documents

Provide the necessary legal adoption documentation as evidence of the adoption.

Same age-based rules apply

All biological child age rules (birth cert, ID, school proof, disability) apply equally to adopted children.

⚖️

Child as Guardian (Loco Parentis)

📌Loco parentis means you are acting in the place of a parent — taking full parental responsibilities for a child who is not biologically or legally yours.

Court Documents (Guardianship Order)

Provide the court documents granting you parental responsibilities for the child.

Standard child age rules still apply

Birth certificate / ID depending on the child's age (same as for biological children above).

♿

Adding a Disabled Relative

✅You can add mother, father, brother, or sister who are disabled and under your care.

Relative's National ID Number

Required to create their profile and link them to your SHA account.

Disability Documentation

NCPWD registration card, medical certificate, or disability assessment report from a recognized institution.

Step 3 of 3 — Final Step
Biometrics Verification
Visit a registered SHA agent in person to complete registration
🚨Important: Your SHA cover is NOT active until biometric verification is complete. Don't go to hospital assuming you're covered before finishing this step.
👆
1

Find a registered SHA agent near you

Use the SHA agent locator at sha.go.ke or call 0800 720 601. Agents are at most Sub-County offices and Huduma Centres.

2

Bring your original ID + Application Reference

Carry your original National ID card and the Application Reference Number from Step 1. A photocopy is NOT accepted.

3

Complete biometric capture (fingerprints + photo)

The agent captures your fingerprints and photograph. This is the final step in the registration process.

4

Dependants under 18 need biometrics too

All dependants under 18 that you added in Step 2 must also attend in person for biometrics capture.

5

Make your first M-Pesa contribution

Lipa na M-Pesa → Paybill → Business No: 222222 → Account: your ID number. Cover activates within 24–48 hrs.
