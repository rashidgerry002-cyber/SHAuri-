My SHA Claim Was Rejected

Find out exactly why, what to do, and get a ready-to-send complaint letter — in minutes.

🚨

Facing an emergency and being turned away?

Call SHA Provider Hotline immediately: 0800 720 601 (free, 24/7). Ask for "emergency member authorization." Hospitals cannot legally deny emergency care.

Member Not Eligible

Pre-Authorization Required

Not Accredited Facility

ERR-07
Duplicate Claim
SHA says a claim for the same service has already been submitted.
ERR-08
Delayed Activation
You registered and paid but coverage hasn't activated in the system yet.
⚠️

Member Not Eligible

Your account is registered but your coverage is inactive.

1
Check your last M-Pesa contribution. Go to M-Pesa → Statements and confirm payment to Paybill 222222.
2
Log into sha.go.ke → "My Account" → Check your contribution history and account status.
3
If contributions show but status is inactive, call 0800 720 601 and say: "My contributions are showing but my account says not eligible. Please do a manual status update."
4
If employed, check with your HR that NHIF/SHA payroll deductions have been updated. Your employer may still be paying old NHIF instead of SHA.
❓

Member Not Found

SHA's database doesn't recognize your ID.

1
Try looking yourself up at sha.go.ke using your ID number to confirm whether you're in the system.
2
If not found, you'll need to register. Use our Registration Wizard to walk through the process step by step.
3
If you're certain you registered, call 0800 720 601. Your registration may have failed silently — a common issue. Bring your ID and registration confirmation SMS if you have it.
🚫

Benefit Limit Exhausted

You've reached the cap for this type of service.

1
Log into sha.go.ke to check your remaining benefits per category (outpatient, inpatient, maternity, etc.).
2
For inpatient/surgery: request a benefit review from SHA by calling 0800 720 601. Serious cases can get exceptional authorization.
3
Consider SHA's enhanced benefit program — additional top-up coverage available via approved private insurers.
📋

Service Not Covered

This procedure isn't in SHA's Essential Benefits Package.

1
Ask the hospital to recode the claim. Sometimes services are billed under wrong codes. A related covered procedure code might apply.
2
Request the hospital to submit a clinical justification to SHA if the service was medically necessary.
3
File an appeal using our Letter Generator below, citing medical necessity and requesting exceptional coverage.
📝

Pre-Authorization Required

SHA needs to approve this procedure in advance.

1
Ask your doctor to submit a pre-authorization request on your behalf through the SHA provider portal.
2
Pre-auth typically takes 24–72 hours. For emergencies, call 0800 720 601 for same-day authorization.
3
Keep the pre-auth reference number. Present it at the hospital when you return for the procedure.
🏥

Not Accredited Facility

SHA doesn't recognize this hospital in their system.

1
Use our Hospital Finder to locate an accredited SHA hospital near you.
2
If you believe this hospital should be accredited, ask their billing department to confirm their SHA provider number.
3
If you paid out of pocket at a non-accredited hospital due to emergency, you can apply for SHA reimbursement — call 0800 720 601.
🔄

Duplicate Claim

SHA's system shows this claim was already submitted.

1
Ask the hospital's billing department to pull the original claim submission record and confirm whether the first claim was actually paid.
2
If the original was paid to the hospital but you still got a bill, that is a hospital billing error — escalate to hospital management.
3
If neither claim was paid, request SHA to void the duplicate flag by calling 0800 720 601 with your claim reference numbers.
⏱️

Delayed Activation

Payment received but coverage not yet reflected in the system.

1
Wait 24–48 hours after payment before trying again. SHA's system updates overnight in most cases.
2
For urgent care that can't wait: call 0800 720 601, give your ID number and M-Pesa transaction code, and request manual activation override.
3
Keep your M-Pesa payment confirmation SMS as proof. Send it via WhatsApp to 0800 720 601 if available.

📅 The SHA Appeal Process

If you've been wrongly rejected, here's the official escalation timeline.

📞
Day 1 — Immediately
Call SHA Helpline
Call 0800 720 601 (free). Explain your rejection and request a claim review. Get a reference/ticket number. This is mandatory before writing any formal letter.
✉️
Day 2–3 — If call fails
Submit a Written Complaint
Use our letter generator below to create a formal complaint letter. Submit it at the nearest SHA office or email [email protected]. Keep a copy for yourself.
🏢
Day 7–14 — Escalation
SHA Must Respond Within 14 Days
By law, SHA must acknowledge your complaint within 7 days and resolve it within 14 working days. If no response, escalate to the SHA Complaints & Appeals Committee.
⚖️
Day 21+ — Final Escalation
Health Cabinet Secretary / Ombudsman
If SHA fails to resolve: file a complaint with the Commission on Administrative Justice (Ombudsman) at ombudsman.go.ke, or contact the office of the Health CS.
