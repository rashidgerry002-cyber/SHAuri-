The Problem

SHA promised universal coverage. Here's what went wrong.

😤

Registration Nightmare

The SHA portal crashes, rejects valid IDs, and gives no clear error messages. Most people give up after 3 attempts.

🏥

Rejected at the Gate

Registered members show up to hospitals and are told their SHA isn't active — with no explanation why.

💸

Middlemen & Scams

Desperate people pay agents Ksh 200–500 to "help" with free registration. Many are scammed.

📄

No Plain-English Guide

SHA's official communication is complex PDF circulars in legal English. The average mwananchi is completely lost.
